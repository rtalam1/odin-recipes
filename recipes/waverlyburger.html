Waverly Burger

Description

The Waverly burger is one of Tacker's Shake Shack's first speciality burgers. The burger was created by Mark Waverly Tacker with the addition of his creation of the trademark waverly sauce. To this day, the waverly remains supreme as the most popular speciality burger at the shake shack.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounce ground beef ball
  • Waverly Sauce
  • Pickle,Lettuce,Tomato
  • Diced Red Onions
  • Brown Sugar and melted butter
  • 4 inch burger bun
  • 2 pieces of cooked bacon
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. First we put the burger on the grill. We then smash the burger into a patty
  2. We will then toast our bun and carmilize our onions with the brown sugar and butter
  3. After the bun is toasted we will dress the bun with waverly sauce, pickle,lettuce,tomato.
  4. Once the burger is cooked, place the cheddar cheese onto the patty. Then place the carmalized onions onto the patty.
  5. Finally, add the bacon and pull the burger off the grill
  6. Then we serve it to the customer. Can't forget the bev, neva neva neva!!!

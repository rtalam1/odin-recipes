Sweet Phoenix!!!

Description

The sweet phoenix is a fantastic sandwich created by Dustin Raper. Dustin found inspiration from the sandwich whenever he created his own unique smoked chicken recipe. The making of the smoked chicken is very unique process and one of the things that makes it unique is that is uses a special raspberry chipotle seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of our house made smoked chicken
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Waverly and Barbecue sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Melted Butter
  • 6 inch hoagie
  • 1 slice of pepper jack cheese
  • 1 slice of colby jack cheese
  • Diced red onions and sliced jalapenos

Directions

  1. Start by placing the smoked chicken onto the grill and then place the onions and jalapenos onto the chicken
  2. Sprinkle the brown sugar onto the chicken and then pour a good amount of melted butter onto everything
  3. Cover the chicken, and then proceed to toast the hoagie
  4. Once the hoagie is toasted, spread the Waverly and Barbecue sauce on the hoagie
  5. Once the chicken is heated up, mix it around and shape it into the form of a hoagie
  6. Place the cheese onto the chicken and then allow it to melt
  7. Once the cheese is melted, placed the sweet phoenix onto the hoagie and then sprinkle the red pepper flakes.

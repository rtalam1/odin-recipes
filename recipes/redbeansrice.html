Red Beans and Rice

Description

Red Beans and rice is very good dish consisting of mainly red kidney beans with any type of rice with a various selection of seasonings. The best way to make the dish is at home, but it can be bought pre made at various places such as walmart or Popeyes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound smoked sausage,sliced
  • 1 onion,chopped,divided
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely diced, divided
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cubes water chicken bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon slat-free garlic and herb seasoning (such as Mrs.Dash)
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning blend
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 (16 ounce) cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (14.5ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups cooked white rice
